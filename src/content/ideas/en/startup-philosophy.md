# The Philosophy of Startups

Building a startup is more than just business; it's about testing a hypothesis about how the world works.

## The Hypothesis

Every startup begins with a belief: "If we build X, then Y will happen." This is fundamentally a philosophical claim about human behavior, economics, and value.

## The Experiment

The market is the laboratory. Customers are the peer reviewers.
